Monkeynomics Flowchart

Prerequisites

  • Follow the farm eco flowchart while doing this.
  • Always get the bare minimum defense so you can greed as much as possible.
  • This only covers the monkeynomics farming flowchart, there are alternative farming flowcharts that are better in certain situations (overclock farming, Ben farming, high eco farming, early IMF all outs).
  • This guide is intended for farming from round 1 to the early 30s, in a real match you can stop farming at any point to rush or defend an all out.

Early Game

Whenever you accumulate enough money (while ecoing) place down a farm and upgrade it to 2-0-0, repeat until you get to the round where you need to get the round 11-13 defense, you typically end up with two to four farms.

Banking up

after preparing vital defenses upgrade one of the 2-0-0 farms into a bank (2-3-0) before round 14 concludes. Eco with grouped yellows until 1000-1500 eco depending on the match-up (stall, map difficulty, aggro) then completely halt your eco, you may skip ecoing or go for 700-900 only in tough matches.

Monkeynomics

Once your bank is close to full (max is 14,000$) upgrade it to IMF loan, 2 rounds later it will be full again (20,000$). Build a second bank with the quality soil cross-path (0-3-1) then cash out the initial IMF and use the IMF ability to upgrade the bank to Monkeynomics (0-5-2), if you do not afford you can sell the initial IMF. Hook up a tech bot to the Nomics or manually use the ability whenever it's available.

Filling up the map

Build two or three BRFs (4-0-2) and when you have enough money sell some of them for a Banana central (5-0-2), you may also use the IMF ability for an early Bcent if you kept the IMF from eariler. Fill up the map with BRFs and for the final farm spot get a Monkey Wall Street (2-0-5).
