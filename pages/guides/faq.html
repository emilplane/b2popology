Eco strategies (no banana farm)

Reds (round 1-2)
Grouped blues (round 3)
Spaced pinks (round 4), you may send blues if you have excess cash
Grouped greens (round 5)
Spaced blacks (round 6)
Grouped yellows (round 7-8)
⏰Slow down eco (send spaced whites or other efficient sends) or pause to afford round 11-13 defense
Mix grouped yellows and grouped pinks (round 13+)
Grouped pinks only at around 1800 eco
Grouped zebras once you reach ~2600 eco if you have no alternative eco source (Village, Sniper, Heli, or Druid)
🛑 Stop ecoing completely whenever you think the game is about to end.
in an lategame eco mirror you might stop in early to mid 30s, against a farm strat you could stop round 27 or 28 to afford BAD defense and a counterrush, on difficult maps you might stop even earlier

Farm Strategies

Reds (round 1-3), you may send spaced blues if you have expensive early defense
Spaced pinks (round 4)
Spaced whites (round 5)
Spaced blacks (round 6)
⏰ Pause your eco round 10 to get defense and farms
Mix grouped yellows and grouped pinks (round 14+)
🛑 Stop ecoing and focus on farming at around 2000 eco
