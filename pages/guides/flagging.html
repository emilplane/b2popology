Flagging

Q: What does it mean for my account to be flagged?
A: Instead of an outright ban, the anti-cheat will issue "flags" which disable some in-game features and let you only play with other flagged users.

Q: How do I tell if my account is flagged?
A: The main menu shows your version number in the bottom right corner, if there's a space between the 'v' and the numbers then the account is flagged.

Q: Why did my account get flagged?
A: Any form of cheating will get you flagged, in addition any noncompetitive behavior such as boosting, surrendering too often, or disconnecting in the lobby many times will lead to a flag. Additionally the anti-cheat is not completely perfect and there will be occasional false positives.

Q: How do I unflag my account?
A: Contact official Ninja Kiwi support
